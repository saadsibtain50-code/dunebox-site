07 Common Questions

Answers, before you ask.

Where exactly is my data hosted?

+
All compute, storage, and inference traffic resides in our Dubai Tier-III data centre. No data is replicated, mirrored, or processed outside UAE borders. We provide written data residency attestation on request.

What is a vGPU slice and how is it isolated?

+
A vGPU slice is a hardware-partitioned share of a physical A10 GPU using NVIDIA vGPU technology. Memory is dedicated and isolated per slice — no other tenant can read your VRAM. Compute is time-sliced fairly. You get root SSH on a dedicated VM that maps to your slice.

How do I access GPUs — is there a portal?

+
Yes. Sign up via our customer portal at dunebox.ai, choose a tier, and you'll have SSH and Jupyter access within minutes. We also provide an OpenAI-compatible REST API for inference workloads.

Do you support custom models or only your own?

+
Bring any open-weight model (Llama, Qwen, Mistral, DeepSeek, Falcon, and more) or your own proprietary weights. We also offer pre-tuned Arabic LLMs as a managed service.

Can I move from a slice to a dedicated GPU later?

+
Yes. Many customers start on an A10 slice for prototyping, scale to a dedicated A10, then move to RTX PRO 6000 or B300 for production workloads. Storage and configurations migrate cleanly between tiers.

Are there data egress fees?

+
No. Unlike hyperscalers, Dunebox does not charge egress fees. You pay only for GPU compute and persistent storage. Network is included.

Can I get compliance documentation for my procurement team?

+
Yes. We provide DIFC compliance attestations, data processing agreements (DPAs), security questionnaires, and architecture diagrams to qualified buyers under NDA.
